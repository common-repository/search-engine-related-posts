== Description ==

When someone is referred from a search engine like Google, the plugin show your blog content matched the terms they search for.

== Installation ==

1. Put <strong>&lt;div style="display:none" id="search_content"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;</strong> at the place in your template where you want the list of related posts
1. Copy search_related_posts.php to direcotry wp-contents/plugin and activate the plugin.
1. done!
